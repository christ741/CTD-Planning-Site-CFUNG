WP4 "Making Website"

  • Wireframe

      • I strated making my own individual wireframe for the homepgae in Adobe FireWorks.
      A wireframe is a blueprint to layout the different section of the website and also
      is a guide for future reference of the website making.


  • Story Board

      • With the wireframe we started to make a story board. A story board is made to give a
      prototype or an better look and vision of the website using and 20/80 rules which
      means making the website with the least amount of time for the most amount of quality.
      We used two software to make the website. "FireWorks" and "DreamWeaver".

      1. FireWorks
        1. index-FW
      2. DreamWeaver
        1. index-DW

      After testing on both programs my conclusion is:

      1. Fire Works
          • Pros
            • Is really simple.
            • Is more open to customize the aperance.
            • Fast to start.
          • Cons
            • Very limited for coding.
            • Very hard to add interations.
      2. DreamWeaver
          • Pros
            • Can make a very interactable website.
            • Very easy to code.
            • It lets you see the final result rigth aways.
            • Very felxible.
          • Cons
            • Very complicated.
            • A lot of fuctions.
            • Needs a lot of researchign to go futher.
